Our Story

A Festival of Flavors

Started in 2018 from a small kitchen in Telangana, Swathi Bakers was born from one woman's dream to share the warmth of handcrafted baking with her community. Today we serve 500+ customers daily — but every single item is still made with the same care and love as the very first loaf.

  • Baked fresh every morning before 6 AM
  • 100% natural ingredients, zero preservatives
  • Custom cakes designed for every occasion
  • Sourced from trusted local farmers in Telangana
