Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver?

Yes! We deliver locally. Same-day delivery for orders placed before 12 PM. WhatsApp us to confirm your area.

How far in advance for custom cakes?

At least 48 hours for standard custom cakes. For elaborate designs (fondant, multi-tier), please give us 5–7 days.

Eggless / vegan options?

Yes! We offer eggless versions of most cakes and cookies. Please mention when ordering. Vegan options available on request.

Can I walk in without ordering?

Absolutely! Walk-ins always welcome. Daily items on the shelf — come early as popular items sell out by noon.

What payment methods?

Cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm) and all major credit/debit cards. 50% advance required for custom orders.

Bulk / corporate orders?

Yes! Special pricing for offices, events, and weddings. WhatsApp or fill the form above. Minimum 2 days notice.

Still Have Questions?

The fastest way to reach us is WhatsApp. We usually reply within 15 minutes during bakery hours.
