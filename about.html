Our Story

From a home kitchen to Telangana's most loved bakery — baked with the same love every single day.

The Beginning

Started with One Oven

"I grew up watching my grandmother bake. The smell of bread rising, the warmth of the kitchen — it wasn't just food, it was love. I wanted to share that with everyone in Telangana."

Swathi started baking professionally in 2018 from her home kitchen, selling to neighbours and WhatsApp groups. Within six months demand outgrew the home — and Swathi Bakers was officially born. Today we operate a full commercial bakery in Siddhartha Nagar, serving 500+ customers every day.

  • Self-taught baker with 10+ years of experience
  • Trained under a French pastry chef in Hyderabad
  • Started with ₹50,000 investment, grew organically
  • Now employs 8 local bakers from the community

Our Journey

From Kitchen to City

2018

The Home Kitchen

Swathi starts baking croissants and cakes from home, selling to neighbours via WhatsApp. First 50 customers in month one.

2019

The First Shop

Opens a small 200 sqft bakery in Siddhartha Nagar. Hires her first two bakers. Queue forms every morning by 7:30 AM.

2021

WhatsApp Orders Boom

COVID forces a pivot to home delivery. WhatsApp ordering explodes. Revenue triples. Custom cake orders surge 400%.

2023

Expanded Bakery

Moves to a 1,200 sqft facility with commercial stone-deck ovens. Team grows to 8. 4.9★ Google rating achieved.

2026

500+ Daily Customers

Telangana's most loved bakery. Serving 500+ customers daily, 35+ menu items, still baking by hand every morning.
